About Mars

Mars, the fourth planet from the Sun, is known as the Red Planet due to its rusty surface colour. Scientists believe it once had water and could have supported life.

Quick Facts

  • Orbit time: 687 Earth days
  • Moons: Phobos and Deimos
  • Average temperature: -63°C
